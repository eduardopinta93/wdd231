Website Plan

Beginner’s Guide to Electric Bass

Site Name

Beginner’s Electric Bass Guide

This name clearly represents a simple, friendly resource designed to help new electric bass players understand the instrument, basic techniques, and essential gear.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to teach beginners the fundamentals of the electric bass. It will provide explanations of the instrument, basic learning techniques, and equipment recommendations, helping new players build confidence as they begin their musical journey.

Scenarios

  • “How do I tune my electric bass as a complete beginner?”
  • “What basic equipment do I need to practice bass at home?”
  • “How should I correctly hold and play the instrument?”

Color Scheme

Primary Color — #1E293B

Used for headings, header, navigation, and important text.

Background Color — #F0F4F8

Used for page backgrounds for readability.

Accent Color — #EAB308

Used for buttons, highlights, and important interactive elements.

Typography

Bebas Neue

Used for titles and headings to provide strong visual hierarchy.

Inter

Used for body text, paragraphs, and general readability across devices.
