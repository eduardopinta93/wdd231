New to Electric Bass?

This site is for complete beginners who just got their first bass and want to understand the instrument, learn basic techniques, and choose the right gear without feeling overwhelmed.

Start Learning the Basics

What You’ll Find Here

Understand Your Instrument

Learn the main parts of the electric bass and what each one does: body, neck, pickups, tuning pegs, and more.

Learn How to Play

Discover basic posture, how to hold the bass, simple finger exercises, and how to practice even if you have limited time.

Choose Beginner-Friendly Gear

Get practical recommendations for cables, small amps, headphone setups, and accessories that make practicing easier at home.

Not Sure Where to Start?

If you feel lost with so much information on the internet, this guide will walk you step-by-step:

  1. Get familiar with the instrument on this home page.
  2. Go to Learn the Basics for tuning, posture, and first exercises.
  3. Visit Gear & Recommendations to build a simple, beginner setup.

You don’t need to know music theory or have previous experience. Just curiosity, patience, and a bit of daily practice.
