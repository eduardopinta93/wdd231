Learn the Basics

Here you’ll learn the fundamental skills every new bass player needs: tuning your instrument, proper posture, finger technique, and simple exercises.

1. How to Tune Your Bass

The standard tuning for a 4-string bass is E – A – D – G.

2. How to Hold the Bass

Keep your back straight, relax your shoulders, and position the bass so both hands can move freely without tension.

3. Basic Finger Exercises

Start with simple 1-2-3-4 exercises on each string to develop finger independence.
